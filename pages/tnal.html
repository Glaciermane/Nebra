Introduction

placeholder

Nea is the perfect multipurpose bot for your server. The bot is community driven and can also be grown with your help.

About this Bot

The bot provides multiple features as moderation and ticket-systems at it's core. The bot is hosted 24/7 and you can be reassured about it's uptime.

Technology

The bot is bulit with node.js using the discord.js library, same with the API the bot uses.

The bot uses it's own API to filter and provide high quality images to your server. With the help of our super fast and flexible API we are able to provide you the best and fastest features.
