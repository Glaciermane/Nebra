Introduction

Greeting adventurer! Your are a Wynncraft player and want to mess with the best of the best?
Get yourself ranked in the official TNA ranking list and compete with others, become a rival and climb the top ranking ladder. This system not just works with the maximum completions, also skill and personality are important aspects to get high ranked. Every classification is done by a vote of multiple players to keep the ranking neutral.

About Nebra

Nebra is a Wynncraft TNA ranking system. The Website is made in html, css and js. The discord bot is currently in development and is written in js using the discord.js library, ruby, sql, python.
